Data Scientist at Argenit Teknoloji

I had the opportunity of working in a multinational environment for 5 months as a data scientist at Argenit Teknoloji in Instanbul. I was there as a part of the AUTOIGG project research team and worked on developing a machine learning methodology for the analysis of time-series data obtained from images acquired by automated fluorescence microscopy. Some of the work activities included:

  • Time series analysis including visualization, augmentation, and normalization of raw data.
  • Development of supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms for time series classification, clustering, and anomaly detection.
Also, I participated in the AUTOIGG workshop at Sile, Istanbul, where I presented the results of my work to colleagues from different fields of research such as Medicine and Biology.
